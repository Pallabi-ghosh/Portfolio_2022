Packitup/JOYN App

Meet your friends & make new friends, whenever you want, wherever you are.

Overview

In my first internship, I worked as a UI/UX Designer and Frontend Developer at JOYN which was a social media start up in Copenhagen. During the internship along with my colleagues I designed and prototyped in Figma. After that I coded the mobile app in react native for iOS and Android devices. It was designed to connect better with user's friends, organize the free time efficiently together, and make real memories instead of posting another social media post for likes.

Role & Responsibilities

  • User Design
  • Prototyping
  • Visual Design
  • React Native
  • Vue JS

My design process

Design

I have sketched low-fidelity wireframes with pencil on paper. After we agreed on the general layouts of our screens, we turned to Figma for our mid-fidelity wireframes. In this case, we created a shared project where we could work on the same file at a single point of time. Always followed the rule of atomic design approach while creating every components.

User flow

Style guide

In our shared Figma file, we compiled all of our components, colors, icons, and typefaces. Later, this helped us to build a style guide to show and instruct others on our final choices for all of the user interface aspects.

Mock ups

Learnings

  • Always keep in mind the problem that you are trying to solve.
  • Research and testing are both very important tools to make sure the design is created with intention.
  • Don’t be too attached to a design idea because that can change rapidly after user testing and make sure you are always prioritising user’s needs.
  • Feedbacks from peers and users are crucial and beneficial for both personal growth and design improvement.
